Cubs will start chewing on meat at about 6 - 8 weeks old, when their teeth are

What kind of animal eats a lion?

Lions have almost no predators. However, old, sick lions are sometimes attacked, killed and eaten by hyenas.
And very young lions can be killed by hyenas, leopards and other predators when they are not being watched carefully
by their mothers. But a healthy adult lion has little to fear from any other animal.

How long does it take a lion to starve to death?

Provided it is well-fed to start with, a lion can go several weeks
without food. It will start to lose condition after about two weeks,
but it can survive for longer and some lions have been known to go a
month without food.

What is a lion afraid of?

Lions (male and female) are afraid of large mammals like giraffes,
hippos and elephants, but they are also afraid of their day-to-day prey.

Can a zebra kill a lion?

What animal can kill a lion?

The other predators such as the leopard, cheetah and hyenas are no match for the strong lion who can kill
a hyena with a bow of its paw. So the lion really does not have many predators although it is hunted in
some occasions: young cubs can be hunted by other predators if left alone.
